Why Screen Printing

Why Screen Printing is a Good Choice

palette

Bright, Bold Colours

The colours are rich and vivid. Your logo or design stands out clearly on any garment.

verified

Lasts for Years

Screen printed designs stay sharp even after hundreds of washes. The print doesn't peel or fade easily.

savings

Cheaper for Bulk Orders

The more you order, the less it costs per piece. Great for schools, companies, and event teams.

dry_cleaning

Comfortable to Wear

The print feels soft on the fabric. There's no stiffness or cracking — it stays comfortable to wear.

Any Design You Like

Simple logo or detailed multi-colour artwork — we print it exactly the way you want it.

speed

Orders Ready on Time
